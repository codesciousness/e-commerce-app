Hal is a headline sans serif typeface with an industrial, utilitarian, condensed design style. Hal is a free typeface and currently only comes in one weight. The typeface includes extended language support, alternate glyphs, arrows, ligatures and more. Hal is a very flexible typeface which can be used for both print and online use.
